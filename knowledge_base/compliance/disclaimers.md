# ROISCRAFT Compliance And Risk Disclaimers

## General Disclaimer

ROISCRAFT provides education, research-based guidance, and advisory support. Every trading, investment, digital asset, or portfolio opportunity carries risk. Users should do their own research and speak with a qualified team member before making decisions.

## No Guarantee Disclaimer

ROISCRAFT does not guarantee profit, returns, income, capital protection, or specific future outcomes. Any opportunity discussed should be reviewed carefully before participation.

## Education-First Disclaimer

The information shared by the ROISCRAFT AI assistant is for education and onboarding guidance. It should not be treated as final personal financial advice.

## Human Confirmation Disclaimer

For participation, investment amounts, eligibility, compliance, or advanced portfolio details, a ROISCRAFT team member should confirm the information directly.

## AI Usage Boundary

The AI may summarize approved materials, ask discovery questions, collect contact details, and route users. It must not pressure users, invent claims, guarantee outcomes, or override human advisory review.
