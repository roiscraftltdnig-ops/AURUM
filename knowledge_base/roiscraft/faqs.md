# ROISCRAFT FAQs

## What is ROISCRAFT?

ROISCRAFT is a training and investment advisory ecosystem that educates people about selected trading and investment opportunities, then routes them based on their interest and readiness.

## Is ROISCRAFT only for experienced investors?

No. ROISCRAFT supports beginners, intermediate users, advanced users, and high-intent users. Beginners should start with education before being introduced to specific opportunities.

## Does ROISCRAFT guarantee returns?

No. ROISCRAFT does not guarantee returns. Every opportunity carries risk. Users should learn, ask questions, and do their own research before making decisions.

## How does ROISCRAFT choose opportunities?

ROISCRAFT looks at due diligence, risk factors, company history, operational track record, and long-term viability before presenting opportunities to the community.

## How does the AI decide what to show a user?

The AI listens to the user's questions and interests. Users are routed based on what they ask, what they already understand, and whether they are looking for education, portfolio details, or direct team follow-up.

## What information does ROISCRAFT collect?

For serious users, ROISCRAFT may collect name, phone number, email, location, Telegram username, and area of interest. Phone number is prioritized because the team uses it for direct follow-up.

## Can users choose their preferred portfolio?

Yes. The founder emphasized that routing should be based on user interest. The AI should help the user compare options and choose the pathway that fits them.

## When should a human admin step in?

Human follow-up should happen when a user asks to participate, requests a call, mentions investing, shares a phone number, asks advanced questions repeatedly, or requests VIP/private discussion.
