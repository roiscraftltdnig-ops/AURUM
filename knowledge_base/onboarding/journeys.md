# ROISCRAFT Onboarding Journeys

## First Message

Welcome to ROISCRAFT. I can help you understand the ecosystem and point you to the right pathway. Are you here to learn the basics, understand Aurum Foundation, explore Bytnet, or speak with the team about participation?

Buttons:

- Learn the basics
- Aurum Foundation
- Bytnet
- Speak with the team

## Beginner Journey

Goal: educate before routing.

Steps:

1. Ask what the user already understands.
2. Give a short explanation of ROISCRAFT.
3. Explain that ROISCRAFT is training and advisory focused.
4. Offer a beginner video, PDF overview, or short explanation.
5. Ask which opportunity interests them.
6. Route to Aurum, Bytnet, broader education, or human follow-up.

Completion signal:

- User understands the basic ROISCRAFT model.
- User chooses an interest path.
- User has viewed at least one beginner resource or requested team contact.

## Intermediate Journey

Goal: help users compare and choose based on interest.

Steps:

1. Ask which opportunity they are researching.
2. Ask what they have already seen or read.
3. Offer portfolio-specific summary, video, or PDF.
4. Explain risk and due diligence principles.
5. Ask whether they want deeper explanation or team follow-up.

Completion signal:

- User identifies a preferred portfolio.
- User receives relevant content.
- User is either nurtured or escalated.

## Advanced Journey

Goal: support deeper due diligence and route serious users.

Steps:

1. Ask what specific concern or question they have.
2. Retrieve trusted knowledge-base documents.
3. Give source-grounded explanations.
4. Avoid unsupported claims.
5. Offer admin follow-up if the user asks about participation, capital, verification, or private discussion.

Completion signal:

- User receives advanced explanation.
- User is routed to documentation, community, or admin follow-up.

## High-Intent/VIP Journey

Goal: collect contact details and escalate.

Triggers:

- "I want to invest."
- "I want to join."
- "How much do I need?"
- "Can someone call me?"
- "I am ready."
- "Send me the details."
- User provides phone number.
- User requests VIP/private discussion.

Steps:

1. Acknowledge intent.
2. Explain that a team member should follow up.
3. Collect name and phone number.
4. Ask for email and country if appropriate.
5. Create admin task.
6. Send Telegram admin alert.

Recommended wording:

"I can help with that. To help the ROISCRAFT team follow up properly, please share your name and phone number. You can also add your email if you want updates and educational materials."
