# ROISCRAFT Founder Discovery Questions

Use these questions for the founder session on May 30, 2026. The goal is to collect everything needed to configure the Telegram AI onboarding, knowledge library, lead qualification, routing, admin reports, and handover structure.

## 1. Business Foundation

1. What is ROISCRAFT in one sentence?
2. What problem is ROISCRAFT solving?
3. Who is ROISCRAFT built for?
4. What should a new person understand first before hearing about any portfolio?
5. What is the parent relationship between ROISCRAFT, Aurum Foundation, and Bytnet?
6. What should the AI say if someone asks, "What exactly is ROISCRAFT?"
7. What should the AI never say about ROISCRAFT?
8. What are the approved public claims about ROISCRAFT?
9. What are the claims that require human confirmation?
10. What is the main action you want every serious user to take?
